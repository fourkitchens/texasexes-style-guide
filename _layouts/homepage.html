About Texas Exes

Since 1885, our core purpose has been to be an independent voice that champions The University of Texas and organizes a formidable network of alumni and friends to change the world. Whether it’s through alumni engagement, enriching the student experience, advocating on the University’s behalf, or communicating the value of UT to the state, the Texas Exes exists to support the University and help it become the top public research university in the country.
